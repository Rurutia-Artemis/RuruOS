**Vision Statement** Artemis Partners was founded in 2024 with a clear mission: to connect the dynamic markets of the Asia-Pacific through strategic investment and cross-border operations. Named after the Greek goddess of the hunt, Artemis embodies our relentless pursuit of opportunity and precision in execution.

What began as a Tokyo-based trading company has rapidly evolved into a multi-vertical group spanning real estate, international trade, food & beverage, technology, and collectibles — with entities in Japan, Hong Kong, and Australia.

**Regional Operations**

**Japan — 株式会社アルテミスキャピタル** Our Tokyo headquarters serves as the nerve center for international trading operations, real estate investment, and TCG collectibles commerce. With deep roots in Japan's business landscape, we leverage local expertise to facilitate cross-border transactions and identify investment opportunities across the region.

**Hong Kong — 月華資本有限公司** Our Hong Kong entity, Moonlight Capital, functions as our gateway to Greater China and Southeast Asian markets. Focused on international trade facilitation and capital advisory, the Hong Kong office connects mainland Asian supply chains with global demand.

**Australia — Umami Lab Group Pty Ltd** Our Sydney operations anchor our Food & Beverage portfolio, including the Mala University (麻辣大学) Sichuan rice bowl franchise and Japanese dining concepts. Sydney also serves as our base for Australian property investment and Oceania market development.

**Timeline** (vertical scrolling, matching DNA Capital's "Our History" section)

- **2024 Q2** — Artemis Capital (株式会社アルテミスキャピタル) established in Tokyo, Japan
- **2024 Q3** — Moonlight Capital (月華資本有限公司) incorporated in Hong Kong, expanding trade operations into Greater China
- **2024 Q4** — Umami Lab Group Pty Ltd established in Sydney; Mala University franchise secured for Australian market
- **2025** — Expansion into AI & technology development, TCG collectibles trading, and property investment portfolio across three markets
- **2026** — Continued growth across all five verticals with plans for Southeast Asian market entry through Singapore base

**Detailed Vertical Descriptions** (each as a full-width section with scroll-triggered animation)

#### Real Estate & Property Investment

We acquire, develop, and manage real estate assets across key Asia-Pacific cities. Our portfolio spans residential and commercial properties in Tokyo, Sydney, and Hong Kong, with a focus on value-add opportunities and long-term capital appreciation. We combine on-the-ground market knowledge with cross-border capital flows to unlock returns that single-market investors cannot access.

#### International Trade & Advisory

Our trading operations form the backbone of the Artemis network. We facilitate import/export transactions spanning consumer goods, specialty food products, beauty and wellness items, and lifestyle brands between Japan, Greater China, Australia, and Southeast Asia. Our advisory practice supports businesses seeking to enter new Asian markets, providing end-to-end consulting from market research and regulatory navigation to distribution and partnership development.

#### Food & Beverage

We operate and invest in dining concepts that bridge Asian culinary traditions with local markets. Our flagship operation is the Mala University (麻辣大学) franchise in Sydney — a fast-growing Sichuan rice bowl brand. We are actively expanding our F&B portfolio with Japanese cuisine concepts and exploring franchise opportunities across Oceania and Southeast Asia.

#### AI & Technology

We develop AI-powered software solutions and provide technology consulting services to enterprises across the region. Our capabilities span system development, data analytics, and digital transformation advisory. We believe AI will fundamentally reshape every industry we operate in, and we are building the tools and expertise to lead that transformation.

#### TCG & Collectibles

Japan sits at the epicenter of the global trading card market. We leverage our position in Tokyo to source, authenticate, and trade premium collectible cards — with a focus on Pokémon and Yu-Gi-Oh! — for collectors, retailers, and investors worldwide. Our operations include wholesale sourcing, international logistics, and digital marketplace development.

## Technical Notes for Implementation

1. **Framework**: Single-page sections with smooth scroll, or multi-page with route transitions (match DNA Capital's Nuxt.js approach). React/Next.js or plain HTML+CSS+JS all work.
2. **Animations**: Scroll-triggered fade-in for each section, animated number counters for data highlights, smooth parallax on hero backgrounds.
3. **Responsive**: Mobile-first, with hamburger nav on mobile.
4. **Assets**: Use placeholder images (dark atmospheric photos — cityscapes of Tokyo, Hong Kong, Sydney at night work perfectly). Team photos use placeholder circles/initials until real photos are provided.
5. **Performance**: Lazy-load images, optimize for fast first paint.
6. **Domain**: artemis-japan.net (or similar)
