## 第一版公开项目页英文文案

### Hero

**TCG Market AI**

**AI-powered market intelligence for Japanese and global trading card markets.**

TCG Market AI is a prototype-stage market intelligence platform developed by Artemis to help card shops, professional resellers, collectors, and TCG investors track pricing trends, buy-list movements, cross-market opportunities, and portfolio value across major Japanese trading card categories.

Our first-stage coverage focuses on mainstream Japanese TCG cards, including Pokémon, One Piece Card Game, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Dragon Ball, and upcoming Naruto-related trading cards.

### Problem

The Japanese TCG market is large, fast-moving, and highly fragmented. Prices and buy-list signals are spread across card shops, marketplaces, auction platforms, social communities, and collector networks.

Card shops, resellers, and collectors need to answer questions such as:

- Which cards are moving up or down today?
- Which shops are increasing buy prices?
- Are there cross-market pricing gaps between Japan and overseas markets?
- Which cards in a personal collection are gaining or losing value?
- What market events may explain sudden price movements?

Today, much of this analysis is still done manually.

### Solution

TCG Market AI combines structured trading card market data with AI-assisted analysis to provide:

- real-time and historical card price tracking
- buy-list and shop price monitoring
- market movement summaries
- cross-market price-gap analysis
- collection portfolio valuation
- AI-generated daily and weekly market commentary
- anomaly and risk alerts for fast-moving cards

The goal is to make TCG market intelligence easier, faster, and more actionable for both businesses and individual collectors.

### Current Stage

We are currently in the prototype stage.

The product is already under development, and the team is importing the first datasets for major Japanese TCG categories. The initial prototype focuses on building the data structure, card database, price-tracking workflow, and AI analysis layer.

A public product page is being prepared, and prototype screenshots or a short walkthrough can be shared as development progresses.

### First Supported Card Categories

The first stage focuses on major Japanese TCG categories:

- Pokémon Card Game
- One Piece Card Game
- Yu-Gi-Oh!
- Dragon Ball trading cards
- Upcoming Naruto-related trading cards

The platform is designed to expand into additional Japanese and global TCG categories over time.

### AI Usage

AI will be used to turn raw market data into useful insights, including:

- summarizing daily card market movements
- explaining why certain cards are rising or falling
- detecting unusual price or buy-list changes
- generating multilingual market reports
- assisting sellers with pricing decisions
- helping collectors understand portfolio value and risk
- creating personalized market alerts and recommendations

### Expected Usage Scale

During the prototype stage, expected AI usage is modest and focused on internal testing, data validation, and early market analysis.

As the product moves into beta, we expect AI usage to grow through:

- automated daily market summaries
- card-level trend explanations
- personalized collection analysis
- seller and shop pricing-assistant workflows
- user-facing market alerts

Japan has a large and active trading card market. In a successful growth scenario, the platform has the potential to serve hundreds of thousands of daily visits, while AI usage will scale based on active analysis and personalized insight requests rather than every page view.

### Why We Need AI Credits

Startup AI credits would allow us to accelerate prototype development and early testing without prematurely limiting AI-powered features.

Credits would be used for:

- testing market-summary generation
- building card-level trend explanation workflows
- evaluating multilingual reports for Japanese and global users
- developing AI-assisted pricing and collection analysis
- prototyping user-facing alerts and recommendations

### Company Context

TCG Market AI is being developed by 株式会社アルテミス, a Japan-based company with business interests in technology, trading, and collectible card markets.

## Demo / 截图建议

建议提供，但不要提供“太粗糙、太空、太像后台半成品”的截图。

更好的 proof pack 是 3 张图：

1. **数据导入截图**：显示已有卡牌类别、数据表、导入进度；隐藏敏感来源和内部路径。
2. **卡牌详情 / 行情页原型截图**：哪怕是 Figma 或本地原型，也要让人看到功能结构。
3. **AI 分析样例截图**：例如某张卡的涨跌原因、市场摘要、风险提醒。

如果界面还不够好，宁可先做一张 Figma/静态 mockup，再配一张真实数据导入截图。这样既可信，又不会显得产品粗糙。
